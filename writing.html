General Rules

  • Language will be either Tamil or English, according to be comfortability of the participants
  • Participants are free to select any topic except for visual writing prompt.
  • In visual writing prompt, the content should be related to the image given in our website and should contain a maximum of 15 lines..
  • Substance for haiku shouldn't surpass 3 lines.
  • Content for the Slogans shouldn't exceed more than 4-5 lines.
  • For the other events, content should not exceed 2 images and they should be clear.
  • Content can be handwritten or typing, based on the writer's creativity.
  • Appropriate words must be used and the contents should be free from any grammatical mistakes.
  • The entries will be evaluated purely based on creativity, writing style, and uniqueness.
