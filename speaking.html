Turncoat
Rules:
  • The individual participant will have to speak both for and against the same given topic alternatively for at least one minute each.
Literary lens
Rules:
  • The participants will be expected to review books/movie/series of their own choice for a maximum of 3 minutes.
Once upon a time
Rules:
  • The participants are expected to talk about any of the given topic for a minute clearly and coherently.
Jam
Rules:
  • The participants are expected to recite a poem of their choice for a maximum of three minutes.

Mirror mirror
Rules:
  • The Participants are expected to tell the stories in an interesting and understandable way for a maximum of about 3 minutes.

General Rules

  • The video should not exceed 3 minutes for the event’s Literary lens (review about books/movie/series), Mirror mirror (story narration) and Jam (poem telling via voice).
  • Make sure that the audio and video are properly synched.
  • For the events Review about book/movie/series, replicate any personality and Story narration participants are free to speak on any topics/books/authors for the events.
  • ● For the events Turncoat and Once upon the time the topics are mentioned in the description.
  • The medium of language must be none other than Tamil or English.
